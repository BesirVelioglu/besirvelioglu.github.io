Keypoint Detection Feature Matching

  • Tech Stack: Python, ORB, SIFT, SURF, Matplotlib Opencv

I aimed to find similarities and matches between two different images using computer vision techniques. I compared the ORB and SIFT algorithms and examined the use of these techniques in various fields. While ORB is preferred in systems with limited hardware resources, such as mobile devices, with its fast and efficient structure, SIFT stands out with its high sensitivity and resistance to scale-rotation changes. These algorithms play a critical role in security areas, especially in identity verification. For example, these techniques are used to determine whether the photo on an identity document matches a real person in biometric security systems. They are also effective in identifying specific people in images obtained from security cameras. In addition, these algorithms are widely used in industrial quality control, environmental perception of autonomous vehicles, medical imaging, and robotic systems. For example, these techniques can be used to evaluate the quality of products in industrial control processes or to identify objects around autonomous vehicles. In the medical field, they play an important place in disease detection and diagnosis processes.
